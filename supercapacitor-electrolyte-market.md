<p><p>Supercapacitor electrolyte market consists of two main types: aqueous solution and organic solvent. Aqueous solution electrolytes are made up of water-based solutions, offering an eco-friendly and cost-effective option. They have a lower energy density but better power performance. On the other hand, organic solvent electrolytes use non-aqueous solvents, providing a higher energy density but lower power capability. They are commonly used in applications requiring higher energy storage. The choice between these types depends on the specific needs of the supercapacitor, balancing between power and energy requirements.</p></p>
