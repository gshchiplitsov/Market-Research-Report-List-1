<p><p>Industrial plastic granulators are used in the plastic recycling industry to break down waste plastic materials into smaller, more manageable pieces. Different types of granulators cater to specific needs. Beside-the-press granulators are compact and placed next to plastic injection molding machines for immediate recycling. Central granulators are larger and used for more extensive recycling applications. Heavy-duty granulators are designed for high-volume processing. Thermoforming granulators specialize in recycling thermoformed plastics. Each type serves a unique purpose in the plastic recycling industry, providing efficient and effective solutions for waste management.</p></p>
