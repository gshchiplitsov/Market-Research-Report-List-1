<p><p>OCA (Optical Clear Adhesive) Films are used in various industries, especially in the manufacturing of electronic devices with displays, such as smartphones and tablets. There are two types of OCA Films: Substrate-Free Optical Adhesive and Substrate Optical Adhesive. </p><p>Substrate-Free Optical Adhesive is a film that can be directly applied to the display without the need for a substrate. It offers high optical transparency and excellent bonding strength, providing a clear and seamless display experience.</p><p>On the other hand, Substrate Optical Adhesive is a film that requires a substrate material, usually made of glass or plastic, to be applied to the display. It provides a strong bond between the display and the substrate, ensuring durability and stability.</p><p>Both types of OCA Films play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and appearance of electronic devices with displays, offering different benefits depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
