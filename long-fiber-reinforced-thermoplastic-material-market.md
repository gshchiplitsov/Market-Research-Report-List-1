<p><p>Long Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (LFT) materials are highly versatile and offer exceptional strength, stiffness, and impact resistance. LFT-G is a type of LFT material that uses glass fibers as reinforcement, providing excellent mechanical properties suitable for applications requiring strength and high temperatures. LFT-D, on the other hand, utilizes carbon or glass fibers combined with thermoplastic matrix, resulting in enhanced strength-to-weight ratio, stiffness, and improved impact resistance. Both LFT-G and LFT-D are widely used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction due to their superior performance characteristics and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
