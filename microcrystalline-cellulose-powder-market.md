<p><p>The microcrystalline cellulose powder market is classified into two types: refined wood pulp based and refined cotton based. Refined wood pulp based microcrystalline cellulose powder is derived from wood pulp and offers properties like improved stability and enhanced binding capacity, making it suitable for various applications such as pharmaceuticals and food additives. On the other hand, refined cotton based microcrystalline cellulose powder is made from cotton and provides high purity, making it ideal for applications in the pharmaceutical industry, particularly as an excipient in tablet formulations.</p></p>
