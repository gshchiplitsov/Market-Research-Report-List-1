<p><p>Frameless wiper blades refers to a type of windshield wiper blades that do not have a traditional metal or plastic frame structure, unlike conventional wiper blades. The increasing demand for advanced automotive technologies and the rising focus on improved safety features in vehicles are the primary factors driving the growth of this market. Frameless wiper blades offer several benefits, including enhanced visibility, reduced noise, improved durability, and resistance to harsh weather conditions, making them increasingly popular among consumers.</p><p>Moreover, the growing adoption of electric and hybrid vehicles, which require high-performance wiper blades due to their aerodynamic designs, is contributing to the market's growth. As these vehicles become more prevalent on the roads, the demand for frameless wiper blades is likely to surge.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in materials and manufacturing techniques are further propelling market growth.
