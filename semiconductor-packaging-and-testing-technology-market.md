<p><p>Semiconductor Packaging and Testing Technology Market Types include 3D Packaging, Fan-Shaped Package, and System in Package (SiP). </p><p>3D Packaging involves stacking multiple chips vertically, enabling improved performance and miniaturization. This type offers better thermal efficiency, increased functionality, and reduced power consumption in electronic devices.</p><p>Fan-Shaped Package refers to the integration of multiple chips into a single IC package, ensuring compactness and cost-effectiveness. It optimizes space usage without compromising functionality.</p><p>System in Package combines multiple chips and other electronic components into a single module. It provides enhanced performance, reduced power consumption, and minimized board space, making it suitable for small-sized devices such as smartphones and wearables.</p></p>
