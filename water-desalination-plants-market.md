<p><p>Water desalination plants are used to remove salt and impurities from seawater or brackish water, making it suitable for drinking or irrigation purposes. There are several types of desalination technologies available in the market. Reverse osmosis (RO) uses a membrane to separate salt from water, while multi-stage flash distillation (MSF) and multiple-effect distillation (MED) use heat to evaporate water and condense it back into freshwater. Hybrid desalination plants combine different technologies for improved efficiency. Electrodialysis (ED) uses an electric field to remove ions from water. These technologies are used worldwide to overcome water scarcity and provide a sustainable source of freshwater.</p></p>
