<p><p>The Twin Screw Multiphase Pumps market encompasses three types of pumps: Twin-screw Pump (TSP), Helicoaxial Pump (HAP), and Progressing-cavity Pump (PCP). TSPs use two intermeshing screws to generate pressure and move fluids with high efficiency. HAPs combine a screw rotor with helical blades to enhance fluid flow. PCPs feature a single rotating screw inside a static stator, creating a progressive cavity to move fluids with precision. All three pump types are widely used in various industries due to their ability to handle multiphase fluids efficiently.</p></p>
