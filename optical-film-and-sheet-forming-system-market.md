<p><p>The optical film and sheet forming system market comprises two types: contact and contactless systems. Contact systems involve direct contact between the film or sheet material and the forming system, which may include pressure or heat. Contactless systems, on the other hand, use non-contact methods such as air or vacuum pressure to mold the material. Both types aim to shape optical films and sheets for various applications, but differ in their approach to forming the material.</p></p>
