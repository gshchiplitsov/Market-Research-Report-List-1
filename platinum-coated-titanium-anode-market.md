Pipe anodes are cylindrical tubes primarily used for cathodic protection. Mesh anodes consist of a connected network of interwoven wires and are commonly employed in wastewater treatment. Rod anodes are solid cylindrical bars utilized in various electroplating processes. The 'Others' category encompasses different forms based on specific requirements. These diverse anode types cater to various industries, including electrochemical, water treatment, and metal finishing.</p></p>
