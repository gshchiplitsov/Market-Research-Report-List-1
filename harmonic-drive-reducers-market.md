<p><p>Harmonic drive reducers are mechanical devices used to reduce the speed and increase the torque of an input shaft. There are three main types of harmonic drive reducers - cup style, hat style, and pancake style. Cup style reducers have a circular cup-shaped flex spline with external teeth, while hat style reducers have a hat-shaped flex spline with internal teeth. Pancake style reducers have a flat shape with an integrated wave generator. Each style has its own advantages and is suited for different applications based on their size, torque capabilities, and mounting options.</p></p>
