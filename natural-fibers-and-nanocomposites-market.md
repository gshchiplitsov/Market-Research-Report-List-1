<p><p>The natural fibers and nanocomposites market can be categorized into four main market types: injection molding, compression molding, pultrusion, and others. Injection molding involves injecting molten material into a mold cavity, which then solidifies to create a desired shape. Compression molding, on the other hand, entails heating and compressing the material within a mold to form the desired product. Pultrusion is a continuous process where reinforcing fibers are saturated with a polymer resin and then pulled through a heated die to create a composite profile. Lastly, the "others" category includes various manufacturing methods not explicitly mentioned.</p></p>
